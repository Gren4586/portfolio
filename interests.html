My favorite past-time is playing video games! All the different stories and mechanics to get immersed in are wonderful. My top three favorite games are:

The Battle Cats
A tower-defense game for mobile phones developed by Japanese company PONOS. Take over the world with weird and cute cats! It features a massive cast of characters, fun visuals and music, and so much to do you almost can't run out!
Hollow Knight
A metroidvania made by the geniuses at Team Cherry, an indie development team based in Australia. Explore a sprawling kingdom past its prime, meeting unique characters and fighting powerful foes as you try to remember your place in this world. Hollow Knight features some of the best combat mechanics I've had the pleasure of experiencing, every challenge pushes you to get better or teach you a lesson. It even has a sequel in the works!
Kirby's Epic Yarn
A platformer developed by Good-Feel and Nintendo. It follows the story of Kirby as he gets sucked into a world made entirely out of arts and crafts, and must stop the evil yarn wizard Yin-Yarn! It's one of the earliest games I remember playing, and I have it to thank for introducing me to the wonders of video games.
